What is AI Love Anime?

AI Love Anime is a web application that provides AI-generated anime recommendations to users. With this app, users can get personalized anime recommendations based on their existing anime list.

Why AI Love Anime?

It feels like we spend more time looking for something to watch than actually watching it. Like they say, no one knows you like you do so I took that to heart. Now recommendations of anime will be based off what you watched and enjoyed. Using AI to draw the invisible lines of similarities in the shows you watch you can easily get new amazing shows that you'll enjoy watching.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: React, Redux for state management, Axios, , Material UI for base styles
  • Backend: Node, Express, Chat GPT Api, MongoDB, PassportJS
  • External APIs: Chat GPT, Myanimelist, Jikan
